# Review request script — use after every job

Use in person, by SMS, or in a follow-up email. Then send your **Google review link** (from GBP → Share review form / "Get more reviews").

---

## In person (short)

> "If you're happy with how everything looks, it would mean a lot if you could leave us a quick Google review—it really helps other folks in Boca find us. I can send you the link right now."

---

## SMS (send right after job)

**Option A:**

> Thanks again for having us! If you have a moment, a Google review helps us a ton: YOUR_GOOGLE_REVIEW_LINK. We really appreciate it.

**Option B (shorter):**

> Your TV is all set. We’d love a quick Google review when you have a sec: [LINK]. Thank you!

---

## Email follow-up (if no review after 3–4 days)

**Subject:** Quick favor — Google review

> Hi [Name],
>
> Hope you’re enjoying the new setup. If you have 2 minutes, a Google review would help us a lot and help other homeowners in Boca find us: YOUR_GOOGLE_REVIEW_LINK.
>
> Thanks again,
> [Your name]

---

**Checklist:** Replace `YOUR_GOOGLE_REVIEW_LINK` with your actual GBP review link. Send within 24–48 hours after the job. One polite follow-up only if they don’t leave a review.
