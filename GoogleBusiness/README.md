## 2. Review acquisition strategy (target: 100+ reviews)

Reviews are one of the strongest ranking and conversion factors for local pack.

| Lever | Action |
|-------|--------|
| **Volume** | Ask every satisfied customer; aim for **100+ reviews** as a milestone. |
| **Timing** | Request within 24–48 hours after job completion, while experience is fresh. |
| **Channel** | Send the **direct Google review link** (from GBP) via SMS or email so one tap opens the review form. |
| **Follow-up** | If no review after 3–4 days, one polite follow-up (SMS or email). |
| **No incentives** | Do not offer discounts or gifts for reviews; keep it compliant with Google policy. |

**Rough pace:** ~2–4 reviews per week with consistent jobs → 100 reviews in about 6–12 months. More jobs + consistent asks = faster.

## 3. Script to request reviews after each job

Use this (or a short variation) in person, by SMS, or in a follow-up email:

**Short script:**

> "If you're happy with how everything looks, it would mean a lot if you could leave us a quick Google review—it really helps other folks in Boca find us. I can send you the link right now."

Then send the **Google review link** from your GBP (Share review form / “Get more reviews” link).

**Even shorter (SMS):**

> "Thanks again for having us! If you have a moment, a Google review helps us a ton: [link]. We really appreciate it."

## 4. Continuous photo upload process from installations

Photos signal relevance, recency, and quality to Google and to customers.

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| **On every job** | Take 3–6 photos: before, during (optional), after. Include wide shot of mounted TV and at least one detail (e.g. hidden wires, clean finish). |
| **Same day or within 24h** | Upload to Google Business Profile (Photos). Add a short caption if helpful (e.g. “65" above fireplace, concealed wiring”). |
| **Weekly** | Aim for at least one new upload per week; more if you have multiple jobs. |
| **Reuse** | Use the same photos (with permission) on landing page, ads, and social to keep messaging consistent. |

**Types to prioritize:** large TVs mounted, hidden wires, before/after, soundbars, elegant rooms. Quality and consistency matter more than quantity per post.

---

## 5. Weekly Google Business posts

Regular posts support relevance and engagement; Google uses this as a freshness and engagement signal.

| Cadence | Action |
|---------|--------|
| **Frequency** | At least **1 post per week** (e.g. every Monday or after a strong job). |
| **Content ideas** | Same-day service reminder, “Just completed” with a photo, tip (e.g. “Why hide TV wires”), seasonal offer, or short customer testimonial snippet. |
| **Format** | Use image + short headline + 1–2 sentences + button (e.g. “Call now” or “Get a quote”). |
| **Link** | Send to landing page or phone number. |

Keep copy short and benefit-focused; rotate between offers, social proof, and tips.
