# Paid Traffic — Google Ads | TV Mounting Boca Raton

Estratégia para **criar e manter** campanhas de busca que geram leads qualificados. O **Google Business Profile** (mapa, reviews, posts) está em [`../GoogleBusiness/README.md`](../GoogleBusiness/README.md); mantenha **landing, telefone e mensagem** alinhados ao GBP.

---

## Manutenção contínua (após o lançamento)

| Frequência | Ação |
|------------|------|
| Semanal | Relatório de **termos de pesquisa**; adicionar negativas; checar impressões e CPC por grupo |
| Semanal | Conferir orçamento e horários; ajustar lances por cidade se usar ajustes de localização |
| Mensal | Revisar taxa de conversão e CPA; testar novas headlines/descrições em RSAs |
| Trimestral | Revisar palavras-chave e oferta; escalar orçamento se CPA e fechamento estiverem estáveis |

Checklist de setup e extensões: [`campaign-checklist.md`](./campaign-checklist.md).

---

## 1. Why Google Ads

Users are already **searching** (e.g. "tv mounting near me"). That lead is closer to buying than on social.

---

## 2. Campaign structure

| Setting | Value |
|---------|--------|
| **Type** | Search Campaign |
| **Goal** | Leads |
| **Location** | 50-mile radius, center **33433** |
| **Hours** | 8:00 AM — 8:00 PM |

Prioritize (when possible) the cities listed in [ACTION_PLAN.md](../temp/ACTION_PLAN.md).

---

## 3. Bidding strategy

1. **Initial phase:** Maximize Clicks (get volume and data).
2. **After enough conversions:** switch to **Maximize Conversions** (or tCPA if you set a cost-per-lead target).

---

## 4. Keywords

### Primary

- tv mounting near me
- tv installation near me
- tv wall mounting service
- tv mounting boca raton

- how to

## 6. Initial budget

| Item | Value |
|------|--------|
| **Daily budget** | $40/day |
| **Monthly budget** | ~$1,200 |
| **Expected CPC** | $3 – $8 |
| **Clicks/month (est.)** | 150 – 350 |

---

## 7. Conversion and close rate

| Metric | Estimate |
|--------|----------|
| Conversion rate (click → lead) | 10–20% |
| e.g. 200 clicks | 20–40 leads |
| Close rate | ~40% |
| Clients (e.g. 40 leads) | 8–16 clients |

---

## 8. Estimated revenue

- **Average ticket:** ~$350
- **Monthly revenue (example):** $2,800 – $5,600

---

## 9. Scale

When numbers validate (stable CPA and close rate):

- Increase budget to **$80 – $150/day**.
- Keep or refine targeting (areas, hours, keywords).

---

## 10. Call script (qualification)

When the customer calls, ask:

- TV size?
- Wall type?
- Do you want wires hidden?
- Soundbar installation?

This allows **quick price estimate** and lead qualification.

---

## 11. Upsells (increase average ticket)

| Service | Add-on |
|---------|--------|
| TV mounting base | $299 |
| Soundbar install | +$120 |
| Wire concealment | +$150 |
| Home theater setup | by scope |

Offer on the call or in post-sale to increase revenue per client.
