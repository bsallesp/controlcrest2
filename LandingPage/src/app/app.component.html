Straightforward rates

Every total below includes labor plus the wall mount (bracket) hardware we install — not labor-only pricing. Rates vary by TV size, cable option, and bracket style (fixed, tilt, or full motion). Most jobs land in the $300–$600 range; we confirm everything before we drill.
