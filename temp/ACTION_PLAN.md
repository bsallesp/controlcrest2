# Action Plan — Qualified Leads | TV Mounting Boca Raton

Goal: generate **qualified TV installation leads** in Boca Raton via paid traffic and local presence.

---

## Where to start

**Phase 1 — Foundation (do this first)**

| Order | Step | Why first |
|-------|------|-----------|
| **1** | **Google Business Profile** — Create or claim the profile, set categories, name, description, service area, hours, phone. | You need a live GBP before ads and before you can collect reviews or send the review link. |
| **2** | **Landing page** — One page with hero, services, benefits, photos, phone + form. | Ads and GBP need a URL; the page is where leads land. |
| **3** | **First 10–15 photos** — Upload existing job photos to GBP (or plan to shoot on next jobs). | Profile looks active and trustworthy from day one. |
| **4** | **Google Ads** — Create Search campaign, keywords, ads, $40/day budget, link to landing page. | Starts paid lead flow while you build organic (reviews, posts). |
| **5** | **Review process** — Save your Google review link; use the script after every job; send link by SMS. | First reviews compound into 3-Pack visibility. |
| **6** | **Weekly posts** — Schedule 1 post per week (offer, tip, or “just completed” with photo). | Keeps profile fresh and supports local SEO. |

Start with **step 1** (Google Business Profile). Once it’s live, do **step 2** (landing page), then **3** and **4** in parallel if you can. **5** and **6** are ongoing from the first job and first week.
